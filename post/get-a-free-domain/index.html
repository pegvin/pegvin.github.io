Get a Free Domain

Thursday, 23 October 2021

You have decided to create your website on the Internet. To do it, you must have a domain name suitable for your purposes, But you are broke and you don’t have money to pay for your domain name?

In this post i will tell you how you can get a domain name for your website at 0 cost!

So firstly Goto Freenom.com and Register And verify yourself

After Registering You should see something like this:

In this interface, click on the Services Drop down menu on the top right:

In the drop down menu click on the Option “Register New Domain”, and you will be brought to a page like this:

Just search for the domain name you want ( make sure you don’t add the .com or something in the end ), and then click on “Search Availability”

And all the available domains will be listed!

The domain name marked as “Free” are available for free and you can select that domain and click on “Checkout”, and you’ll be brought to a page like this:

Freenom gives the user first 12 months / 1 year that domain for free! just select the maximum time free available from the drop down menu, for me currently it’s 12 months, maybe they will increase or decrease that in future

Click on continue and you will be brought to this page:

make sure to click on “I have read and agree to the Terms & Conditions” checkbox and click on “Complete order” button.

Hurray! you just got a free Domain name for your website!, to use your new domain name just goto freenom homepage and click on “Sevices” drop down menu and click on “My Domains” and click on “Manage Domain” and add your CNAME or A Records!
